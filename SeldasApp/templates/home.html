Our Story Begins

The story of this café began with a deep passion for the art of coffee. Driven by the desire to create the perfect cup, the journey started with sourcing the finest beans and learning the craft of brewing to perfection. Every detail was carefully considered, from the roasting process to the brewing techniques, with a focus on delivering bold, rich flavors that awaken the senses. The café quickly became known for its exceptional taste, attracting coffee lovers who appreciated the complexity and depth of each cup . Today, it remains a place where the love for great coffee is at the heart of every experience, bringing people together over the ultimate brew..

Our specials

A smooth blend of rich espresso, steamed milk, and creamy foam, topped with a touch of vanilla and a sprinkle of cinnamon. Perfectly crafted to warm your soul.

At Selda’s, the macchiato is a masterpiece of simplicity and flavor. A shot of bold, aromatic espresso is gently “stained” with a touch of velvety steamed milk.
